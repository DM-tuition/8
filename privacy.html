Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026

DM Tuition ("we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, and how we look after it.

What we collect

When you submit an enquiry through our website, we collect the information you choose to provide, which may include:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your contact / phone number (optional)
  • The subject you're interested in and any message you send us

How we use it

We use this information solely to respond to your enquiry, arrange tutoring sessions, and communicate with you about our services. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

How enquiries are processed

Our contact form is handled by Formspree, which forwards your message to our email inbox. Their handling of data is governed by their own privacy policy.

Analytics

We may use privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics to understand how visitors use the site in aggregate. This does not identify you personally.

Data retention

We keep enquiry information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and respond to your request, after which it is deleted.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at DMtuition@gmail.com.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, email DMtuition@gmail.com.
